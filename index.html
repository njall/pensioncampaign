The objective of the AEA Technology Pension Campaign is to secure the pension rights of scheme members that may be otherwise lost because AEA Technology entered a pre-pack administration. If you are a member of the AEA Technology pension scheme, we urge you to look closely at how badly you will be affected if your pension is reduced to Pension Protection Fund (PPF) levels.
